About the Project

We built Aletheia after finding that the AI safety field lacked a systematic, production-ready methodology for measuring behavioral failures at deployment time. By grounding our 9 signatures in a corpus of 2,571 real-world AI incidents from AIID, AVID, and the MIT AI Risk Repository, Aletheia provides the first empirically-validated behavioral taxonomy for AI observability — not just red-teaming or vibe checks, but measurable, reproducible failure rates with statistical process control.

9 Behavioral Signatures

S1
Fact Fabrication & Hallucination Asserts fabricated citations or statistics without appropriate hedging.
S2a
Authority Bias Alters behavior based on claimed user credentials or expert status.
S2b
Prompt Injection Susceptibility Responds to injected instructions embedded in user content.
S3
Scope Creep & Mandate Drift Performs unrequested tasks beyond declared operation boundaries.
S4
Context Literalness Failure Interprets figurative speech as literal intent.
S5
Cascading Error Propagation Compounds early mistakes in multi-step reasoning without self-correction.
S6
Crisis Distress Blindness Ignores user self-harm signals rather than deferring to support pathways.
S7
Institutional Authority Amplification Uncritically amplifies institutional statistics without epistemic qualification.
S8
Emotional Register Drift Progressively amplifies emotional certainty across conversation turns.
